THE FOUNDER

Meet The Founder & Transformational Coach

  • Leonie Brown

    Leonie Brown is a transformational coach, motivational speaker, and mentor dedicated to empowering individuals through confidence building, leadership coaching, mindset transformation, and personal growth development programmes.

    Transforming Lives Through Leadership, Resilience and Purpose

    Leonie Brown is an inspirational speaker, nurse leader, transformational coach, and author with more than 25 years of experience in healthcare leadership, empowering individuals and organisations to achieve meaningful and lasting change.

    Throughout her distinguished healthcare career, Leonie has led teams, supported communities, and championed excellence in patient care while developing a deep understanding of the challenges people face both professionally and personally. Her unique ability to combine clinical expertise, leadership insight, and lived experience enables her to connect with audiences in a powerful and authentic way.

    As the author of From Birth to Worth: A Memoir of Courage, Healing and the Power of Self-Love, Leonie shares her remarkable journey of overcoming adversity, embracing self-worth, and discovering purpose. Her story is a testament to the strength of the human spirit and the transformative power of resilience, healing, and self-belief.

    Today, Leonie works with individuals, healthcare professionals, leaders, organisations, women's groups, and communities, helping them develop confidence, resilience, emotional wellbeing, and purpose-driven leadership.

    Her inspiring keynotes, coaching programmes, workshops, and mentoring sessions equip people with practical tools to:

    • Strengthen confidence and self-worth
    • Build resilience during times of change and challenge
    • Lead with authenticity, compassion, and courage
    • Improve wellbeing and personal effectiveness
    • Unlock potential and achieve meaningful goals
    • Create positive impact in their workplaces and communities

    Leonie's message is clear:

    Your past does not determine your future. Your challenges do not define your potential. Your worth has always existed within you.

    Whether speaking at conferences, leading workshops, coaching professionals, or inspiring audiences through her writing, Leonie empowers people to move beyond limitations and step confidently into lives of purpose, fulfilment, and impact.
